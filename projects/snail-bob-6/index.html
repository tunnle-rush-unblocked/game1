In Snail Bob 6, you need to help Bob conquer a series of vertical levels packed with new surprises, clever puzzles, and interactive tools. Tap switches, operate gadgets, and manipulate the environment to ensure Bob stays safe and reaches the top in one piece!

How to Play?

  • Explore & Identify – Survey the level. Spot buttons, moving parts, and potential hazards.
  • Act & React – Tap switches, drag levers, drop platforms—experiment until Bob has a clear path.
  • Watch Bob – Time your moves right—one ill-timed action, and Bob might slip, fall, or get squished!
  • Collect & Replay – Grab all stars on your first run, or replay levels to uncover secrets.
