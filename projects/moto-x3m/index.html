Moto X3M Unblocked

Moto X3M is an online bike racing game. In this game, the goal is to race your bike through levels with big moving obstacles you need to jump over or avoid. You can do flips in the air to finish faster and get a perfect score. Try to achieve levels in the shortest time possible. And, you can do it by playing more and more.


Moto X3M bike race game is the first release in the series of Moto X3M that include Moto X3M 2, Moto X3M Winter, Moto X3M Pool Party and the latest Moto X3M Spooky Land.


How to play Moto X3M?

  • Use the Up Arrow ↑ key to speed up or accelerate the bike.
  • Use the Down Arrow ↓ key to brake.
  • Use the Left ← and Right → Arrow keys to control your motorbike’s position. They’re important for doing front flips and back flips.

Who developed Moto X3M?

Moto X3M series was developed by Madpuffers in 2016. They also developed more games like Football Masters, Volleyball Challenge, Tennis Masters and Basketball Stars.
